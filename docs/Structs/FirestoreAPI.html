FirestoreAPI

struct FirestoreAPI

Contains functions used to read services from and write services to the Firestore.

The Firebase documentation by Google was used as guide during the creation of the Firestore and its functions. SOURCE: https://firebase.google.com/docs/firestore/query-data/get-data SOURCE: https://firebase.google.com/docs/firestore/manage-data/add-data
